Fight for your Life

Welcome to a frustrating game ^.^

Who will you be?

Knight

Honorable and brave, a knight protects their allies and keeps the attention of the enemy.
Specializing in heavy armor and weilding a sword and shield, this makes them very durable with a
high vitaility strength, but low speed and agility. Prefers the thrill of the fight over casting or
long range.

Priest

Using their faith as their core strength, Priest burn their enemies with righteous fire as well as healing your allies in the
same breath. They are a great support, able to give supportive buffs that protect and heal allies.
Priest wear light armor making them very weak vs. physical attacks but can resist spells better then a heavier armor types.
They have high mana as well as an arsenal of supportive spells.

Hunter

While using stealth and guile is prefered, Hunters are also a well rounded brawler type. They can disappear and reappear to deliver
massive damage from the shadows at range. They, for the most part prefer using a bow, being a master of the craft it makes
them very deadly, but can also be a master of other weapons. Hunters wear meduim armor, give them some protection vs. phisical and vs. magical. Some have even
been known to know some simple magics to trick their enemy.

Wizard

Often bizzare and missunderstood, Wizards are very intellegent masters of their craft. From the Elements, to Time and Space,
they do not use a phsical weapon, instead they cast powerful spells that decimate the enemy, leaving conditions on the ones
unlucky enough to survive their initial strike. Wizard wear light armor, making them very weak vs. phisical attacks but can
resist spells better. Wizards are the strongest with weilding offensive magic.
