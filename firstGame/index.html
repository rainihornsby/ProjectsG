Who will you be?

Knight

Honorable and brave, a knight protects their allies and keeps the attention of the enemy.
Specializes in heavy armor, weilding a sword and shield, this makes them very durable with a
high vitaility, strength and endurence, but low speed and agility. Has no Magical talent.

Priest

Using their faith as their core strength, Priest burn their enemies with righteous fire as well as healing your allies in the
same breath. They are a great support, able to give supportive buffs that protect and heal allies.
Priest wear light armor making them very weak vs. phisical attacks but can resist spells better then a heavier armor type.
They have high mana as well as an arsenal of supportive spells.

Hunter

Using stealth and guile, Hunter are a well rounded brawler type. They can disappear and reappear to deliver
massive damage from the shadows at range. They for the most part use a bow, being a master of the craft it makes
them very deadly. Hunter wear meduim armor, give them some protection vs. phisical and vs. magical. Some have even
been known to know some simple magics that trick their enemy.

Wizard

Often bizzare and missunderstood, Wizards are very intellegent masters of their craft. From the Elements, to Time and sSpace,
they do not use a phsical weapon, instead they cast powerful spells that decimate the enemy, leaving conditions on the ones
unlucky enough to survive their initial strike. Wizard wear light armor, making them very weak vs. phisical attacks but can
resist spells better, but is the strongest with weilding offensive magic.
